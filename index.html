About

Production apps, not prototypes.

I'm a Flutter developer focused on shipping real apps that hold up once actual users are on them. My core stack is Flutter/Dart with Bloc/Cubit, a clean, layered architecture (use-cases and repositories), Dio for REST APIs, and full Arabic/English (RTL) localization.

My work spans HR/attendance and logistics/delivery. I'm most comfortable with the harder features — real-time tracking and maps, biometric face & liveness verification, and offline-first flows that stay correct when the network is unreliable. Since June 2026 I've also been working beyond the mobile side — backend development with .NET (ASP.NET Core / ABP), from production bug fixes to building services.

What I care about most is the unglamorous half of shipping: solid validation, error handling, and the production debugging that keeps releases stable.
